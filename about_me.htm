From 2015, I am a Ph.D. student under the supervision of professor Wim Michiels in the NUMA Section at the Department of Computer Science , KU Leuven, Belgium.
The aim of my Ph.D. is to develop and analyze numerical methods for the stability assessment and stabilizaton of linear time-delay systems. The main contributions follow two research directions. First, we consider linear time-invariant time-delay systems, whose parameters are affected by uncertainties, modeled by a random vector. Second, we present novel stability assessment and stabilization methods for linear periodic time-delay systems with commensurable period and delays. From 2017 onwards, I contribute in the Society of Industrial and Applied Mathematics (SIAM) Student Chapter KU Leuven.

I graduated in the Bachelor's Degree in Mathematics at the University of Udine (Italy) in 2013. In 2015, I took part at the Erasmus exchange program at the Norwegian University of Science and Technology (NTNU), Trondheim (Norway). In the same year I graduated in the Master's Degree in Mathematics at the University of Udine (Italy) in 2015.

From 2010 to 2015 I was a student of Scuola Superiore a 5-year board and lodging merit bursary for excellence at University of Udine.
