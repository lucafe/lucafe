Research

My research interests focus on Computational Mathematics and Numerical Analysis and Simulation, and include

  • Uncertain Quantification, Statistics and Probability Theory
  • Dynamical Systems (Time-delay systems)
  • Approximation theory (Polynomial approximation, Integration methods, and Model Order Reduction)
  • Optimization and Control theory
  • Eigenvalues and Eigenvector analysis
  • Effective Graphical Display and Data Visualization
