My PhD project deals with stability optimization of Delay Differential Algebraic Equations whose parameters are described by random vectors. My supervisor is professor Wim Michiels in the NUMA Section at the Department of Computer Science , KU Leuven, Belgium.
From 2017 onwards, I contribute in the Society of Industrial and Applied Mathematics (SIAM) Student Chapter KU Leuven

I graduated in the Bachelor's Degree in Mathematics at the University of Udine (Italy) in 2013. In 2015, I took part at the Erasmus exchange program at the Norwegian University of Science and Technology (NTNU), Trondheim (Norway). In the same year I graduated in the Master's Degree in Mathematics at the University of Udine (Italy) in 2015.

From 2010 to 2015 I was a student of Scuola Superiore a 5-year board and lodging merit bursary for excellence at University of Udine.
